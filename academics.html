Excellence in Education and Holistic Development

Curriculum

We follow the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which is designed to provide a comprehensive and balanced education. Our academic program is enhanced with value-added courses and co-curricular activities to ensure holistic development.

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Focus on foundational skills in languages, mathematics, environmental studies, and creative arts.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Introduction to specialized subjects with emphasis on critical thinking and analytical skills.

High School (Grades 9-12)

Comprehensive preparation for board examinations with science, commerce, and humanities streams.
