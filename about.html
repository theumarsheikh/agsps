About Us

Learn about our history, vision, and commitment to excellence

Our History

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Anil Gupta, A.G. Public School began with a simple mission: to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with character building. What started as a small institution with 50 students has now grown into one of the region's most prestigious educational establishments.

Over the years, we have consistently adapted to changing educational needs while maintaining our core values of integrity, excellence, and holistic development.

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution that nurtures innovative thinkers, responsible citizens, and future leaders who will make positive contributions to society.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that encourages academic excellence, physical fitness, emotional resilience, and social responsibility through a holistic approach to education.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Responsibility, Innovation, and Compassion form the foundation of everything we do at A.G. Public School.

Principal's Message

"At A.G. Public School, we believe that every child is unique and has the potential to excel. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities provide the perfect environment for students to discover their passions and develop their talents.

We are committed to creating responsible global citizens who are prepared to face the challenges of tomorrow. Welcome to our school family."

- Dr. Priya Sharma, Principal
